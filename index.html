Enter decimal degree coordinates in the top box to see the degrees-minutes-seconds equivalent. To convert degrees-minutes-seconds, enter coordinates into the bottom box.

Decimal degrees can be formatted like any of the following:

42.350278, -71.106389
42.350278N, 71.106389W
42.350278 -71.106389

DMS coordinates can be formatted like any of the following:

42N 21' 1", 71W 6' 23"
42N 21 1, 71W 6 23
42 21m 1s, -71 6' 23"
42 21' 1s, -71 6m 23"
42N 21 1 71W 6 23
